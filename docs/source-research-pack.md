## Editorial Use Rules

- Every article must be original writing in the MSG voice.
- Source facts should be paraphrased and interpreted through the MSG thesis.
- Do not quote more than a short phrase unless absolutely necessary.
- Do not fabricate dates, metrics, clients, revenue, product launches or public achievements.
- If a source only supports broad context, the article must stay broad.
- If a month cannot be supported by product-specific evidence, write a founder/research note about the broader capability thesis for that period.
