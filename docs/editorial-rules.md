# MSG Updates Editorial Rules

## Voice

Write like Mustard Seed Group is building an institution, not running a campaign.

The voice should be:

- British English.
- Founder-led.
- Research-aware.
- Quietly technical.
- Confident without hype.
- Specific without exposing private systems.
- Clear enough for public readers and future collaborators.

Avoid:

- Startup clichés.
- Inflated claims.
- Fake milestones.
- Agency sales language.
- Buzzword-heavy AI marketing.
- Over-explaining private boundaries on public pages.

## Historical Articles

Historical articles should be written as if they were authored during that month, but must not invent unsupported facts.

Allowed:

- Period-appropriate reflection.
- Public AI context from credible sources.
- Public-safe founder thesis.
- Public product positioning that is already intended for the site.
- Broad lessons from experimentation, research, performance and execution.

Not allowed:

- Fake launch announcements.
- Fake customers.
- Fake revenue.
- Fake usage numbers.
- Fake open-source traction.
- Private repo contents.
- Private technical implementation.

## Plagiarism Control

Every article must be original prose.

Rules:

- Use sources for facts and chronology, not sentence structure.
- Do not copy headlines from sources.
- Do not copy source paragraphs.
- Do not imitate a source's structure if it is distinctive.
- Keep direct quotation to a minimum and only where necessary.
- Prefer synthesis: what the source means for MSG's capability thesis.

## Public-Safety Checklist

Before an article is published, confirm:

- No private code.
- No prompts or system prompts.
- No runbooks.
- No agent orchestration details.
- No memory schemas.
- No lead scoring or pricing logic.
- No client data.
- No internal workflows.
- No private roadmap.
- No unreleased strategy.
- No secret or credential references.

## Draft Workflow

1. Draft into `/content/drafts/`.
2. Include source links in frontmatter.
3. Mark `status: "draft"`.
4. Mark `reviewed: false`.
5. Keep generated thumbnails empty until the image generation pass.
6. Review for accuracy, originality and public safety.
7. Move to `/content/blog/` only after approval.
