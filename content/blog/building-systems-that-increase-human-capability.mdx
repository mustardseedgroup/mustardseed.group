## A company needs a reason to hold together

Mustard Seed Group is not organised around a single product category.

It is organised around a capability question: how can technology help people become more capable than they were yesterday?

## Four domains

The group builds across intelligence, performance, execution and creativity.

That is why research, business operating systems, human performance tools and creative culture can belong together without becoming a random collection.

## The public layer

This site will share public notes, product updates, research thinking and selected experiments.

The private systems behind the group remain private by design.
