## What Orbit is being built to do

Orbit is being built as a business operating system.

Its public purpose is simple: help teams move from prospect to launched product with less operational drag.

## Why it matters

Businesses often lose momentum between interest, planning, delivery and launch.

Orbit is focused on that space between intent and outcome.

## Public direction

The public direction for Orbit is focused on product philosophy, execution systems and the movement from interest to launched product.
