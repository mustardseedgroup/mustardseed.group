## Technology should not replace people

It should amplify what people are capable of becoming.

Mustard Seed Group exists to build products, research initiatives and systems that help individuals and organisations operate at a higher level.

## Systems over fragments

Features are useful, but systems compound.

The group is interested in products and research that change how people think, move, create and execute over time.

## Research before product

Research creates insight. Products turn insight into systems. Consumer brands test behaviour at scale. Services prove commercial value.

That loop is the group.
