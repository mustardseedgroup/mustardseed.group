## Mission

Mustard Seed Group exists to build systems that increase human capability.

We build tools, products and research that help people and organisations execute at a higher level.

## Origin

Most founders pick one lane.

Chiko Shire kept combining them.

Technology, entrepreneurship, fitness, music, creativity and systems became the raw material for a group built around capability.

## The Name

Great things often begin as small things.

The smallest idea, nurtured correctly, can become something that changes lives.

Mustard Seed Group exists to cultivate those ideas into products, companies and research initiatives that compound over time.

## The Build Thesis

The group builds across four domains: intelligence, performance, execution and creativity.

Those domains make the portfolio coherent. They explain why research, AI agents, business operating systems, human performance tools and creative culture can belong inside the same institution.

This website explains the group, its products, its research direction and selected public updates.
