## Research creates better products

Research gives a product a stronger centre of gravity.

It helps the group understand behaviour, systems and capability before committing to a product surface.

## The loop

Research creates insight.

Products turn insight into systems.

Consumer brands test behaviour at scale.

Services prove commercial value.
