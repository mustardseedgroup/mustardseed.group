## What TUXX is

TUXX builds custom AI systems and internal software for ambitious organisations.

It is the services and commercial systems layer of Mustard Seed Group.

## Pattern Up

Pattern Up exists as an earlier TUXX product layer for follow-up, messaging, pipelines and scheduling.

It is not a standalone Mustard Seed Group pillar.

## Role in the ecosystem

TUXX proves which systems create commercial value in the real world.

Those lessons can inform Orbit, Benediction Lab research and future products.
