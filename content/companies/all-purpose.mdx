## What All Purpose is

All Purpose is the consumer ecosystem inside Mustard Seed Group.

It focuses on performance, creativity, culture and personal development.

## Products

Products and initiatives include CheekyGains, Naira, Relay, Horizon, Made It Out and All Purpose Music.

## Role in the ecosystem

All Purpose turns research and systems thinking into consumer products that meet people inside everyday behaviour.

It is where human performance, communication, life planning, transformation stories and creative culture can become products.
