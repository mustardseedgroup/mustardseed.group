## What Benediction Lab is

Benediction Lab is the research arm of Mustard Seed Group.

It explores AI systems, memory, human behaviour, agentic operating systems, future products and experiments.

## Orion

Orion is a Benediction Lab research project exploring agentic operating systems, memory, GUI control, commercial execution and autonomous product development.

Orion also powers Orbit commercially as its intelligence layer.

## Public work

Benediction Lab shares research notes, concept documents, experiments and public demonstrations that explain the direction of the lab.
