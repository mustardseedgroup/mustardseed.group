## What Orbit is

Orbit is an AI operating system for business.

It is being built to help teams move from prospect to launched product through agents, workflows and execution systems.

## Role in the ecosystem

Orbit productises research and internal operating knowledge into a commercial system.

It sits in the execution layer of Mustard Seed Group.

## Public boundary

The public story can discuss Orbit's purpose, product philosophy and integration examples.

The private product code, orchestration logic, lead intelligence, runbooks, agent memory and internal workflows are not public.
